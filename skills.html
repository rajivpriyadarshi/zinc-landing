What Skills Enable

Skills provide your AI agent with the knowledge and tools to handle payments safely and efficiently. Here's the difference:

Without Skills

1

Agent lacks payment capability

No understanding of how to interact with payment infrastructure or card provisioning systems

2

Requests user credentials

May ask for credit card numbers directly, creating security risks and compliance issues

3

No audit trail

Transactions happen outside controlled systems with no logging, verification, or policy enforcement

4

Security risk

Sensitive payment data exposed to agent context, potential for misuse or leakage

With Skills

1

Checks available balance

Agent verifies account status and available funds before initiating any transaction

2

Creates payment intent

Declares purchase purpose, expected amount, and merchant constraints through structured API

3

Obtains virtual card

Receives a locked, single-use card provisioned specifically for this intent and merchant

4

Completes purchase safely

Transaction executed with full audit trail, receipt capture, and policy enforcement
